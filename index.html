Rules of Ring of Fire


It is important to remember that everyone has played a different version of this game so the rules may differ from group to group but I am sure you can all come to some kind of agreement.

First to set up the game, grab a cup/pan/bowl or any other container that can be used to hold alcohol
Everyone get a drink and take it in turns to pull a card (or tap a phone screen) then follow the rules depending on what card they pull.
Before you start the game you must know what each card stands for:

    • Ace - Waterfall.
      Everyone must keep drinking until the person who picked the card stops. So who knows how long you will be going for!
      2 - You (choose)
      You can choose someone to drink
      3 - Me
      You must drink
      4 - Whores
      All girls drink
      5 - Thumb Master
      When you put your thumb on the table everyone must follow and whomever is last must drink. you are the thumb master till someone else picks a five.
      6 - Dicks
      All guys drink
      7 - Heaven
      Point your finger in the sky, whoever is last must drink
      8 - Mate
      Choose someone to drink with you
      9 - Rhyme
      Pick a word such a dog and the person next to you must rhyme with dog, like log, and it goes to the next person and the next, in a circle, until someone messes up and he or she will have to drink
      10 - Categories
      Pick a category such as card games (It could be a lot more interesting), everyone must go round the circle naming different names of card games. Whoever messes up, drinks.
      Jack - Make a Rule
      You can make up any rule that everyone has to follow, such as you can only drink with your right hand. Everyone (including you) must follow this rule for the whole entire game and if you disobey you must drink.
      Queen - Question Master
      The person who pulled the card is now the question master. If they ask a question and someone else who is playing the game responds, they must drink.
      King - Pour
      You must pour a little of your drink into the nominated container. Whomever picks up the NEXT king must drink the whole thing, which could be filled with different drinks, so who knows how bad it could taste!

    • Now that you know the rules. One person starts off by pulling a card, and whatever the card may be, follow the rule. Go in a circle and keep picking a card till you are too drunk to continue.

    NOTE: These are just the rules I have always played. If you have any suggestions then feel free to get in touch, my contact details are on the info page.
